Discover Accra's Hidden Gems

Experience the vibrant culture, stunning beaches, rich history, and modern attractions that make Accra one of Africa's most exciting destinations.

1.Kwame Nkrumah
Memorial Park

The final resting place of Ghana's first President and a central figure in Pan-Africanism. It's a beautiful, historical park that honors his legacy.Explore more→

2.Makola Market

Immerse yourself in the energy of Accra's main commercial hub. It's a massive, bustling market where you can find everything from fabrics and produce to local crafts. Be prepared for crowds and a sensory overload!Explore more→

3.Labadi Beach

Accra's most popular beach spot. It comes alive on weekends with music, food vendors, cultural performances, and horseback riding. It's great for relaxing or experiencing local weekend beach culture.Explore more→

4. Independence Square
(Black Star Square)

This is one of the most iconic national monuments in Ghana, built to commemorate Ghana's independence in 1957. Explore more→

5.Jamestown Lighthouse

Jamestown is one of the oldest districts in Accra, full of history, colonial-era buildings, and a strong coastal community. The current lighthouse was built in 1932 (replacing an earlier 1871 structure).Explore more→

6. Bojo Beach

A blend of local culture, eco-friendly charm, and modern resort amenities. The boat ride across the river sets it apart, while its clean sands and calm atmosphere make it a favorite for families, couples, and solo travelers seeking relaxation. Explore more→
